Privacy

Plainly: we don't want your data, and our apps are built so we never get it.

Last updated 13 June 2026.

The short version

Synapsin Gravy Workshop is a tiny independent studio in Sacramento, California. This website is a static page — it sets no tracking cookies, runs no analytics, and does not collect personal information about visitors. Our apps are built to keep your data on your own device.

This website

  • No analytics, no advertising, no tracking pixels, no third-party cookies.
  • The site is served by Vercel, our hosting provider, which may keep standard server access logs (such as IP address and request time) for security and operations. We do not use those logs to identify or profile you. See Vercel's privacy policy for details.
  • If you email us, we receive what you send (your address and message) so we can reply, and nothing more.

AppLadle (our Android app)

AppLadle is a personal workspace of small apps that runs on your phone. It is built to be private by default:

  • Your data stays on your device. The apps, notes, and content you create are stored locally on your phone, encrypted at rest. We have no servers that receive them and no account to sign into.
  • No analytics or telemetry. AppLadle does not phone home, and we do not collect usage data.
  • Optional AI generation uses your own key. If you choose to generate an app with AI, AppLadle uses an API key you supply, and your request goes directly from your device to that AI provider (for example, Google's Gemini API). That request is handled under that provider's privacy policy, not ours — we never see it, and the app ships with no key of its own.
  • Your work is portable. The apps you make are plain files you can export and take with you.

If a future version of AppLadle, or another of our apps, ever collects anything, we will update this page before it does and say so plainly.

Children

Our apps are not directed at children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect information from them. Because our apps don't collect personal information at all, there is nothing for us to gather.
